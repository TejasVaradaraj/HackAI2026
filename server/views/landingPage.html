analytics

FinBERT Sentiment Scoring

Every Reddit post is scored using FinBERT (70% post sentiment) and VADER (30% comment sentiment), then aggregated into daily engagement-weighted metrics with churn, advocacy, and trust signals.

show_chart

Sentiment-Stock Correlation

Computes Pearson correlations between AI company sentiment shifts and stock price movements at 1, 2, 3, 5, and 7-day lags — showing when and how sentiment predicts market moves.

LSTM Forecasts & Alerts

A 2-layer LSTM predicts 14-day sentiment trajectories. Z-score anomaly detection flags unusual sentiment spikes, and the Sentiment Momentum Score fires stock impact alerts automatically.
